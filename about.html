About Food Spin

Food Spin is a nutrition-themed website that makes healthy eating more fun and interactive. Instead of choosing the same foods every day, users can spin a wheel and discover new healthy options.

The Food of the Month wheel focuses on larger nutrition goals by highlighting foods that can be added to meals throughout the month. The Food of the Day wheel gives a quick daily suggestion for a snack, meal ingredient, or healthy choice.

Our nutrition philosophy is simple: eat a variety of colorful, balanced foods from different food groups. Fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, whole grains, dairy, seafood, nuts, and legumes all support a healthy lifestyle.
